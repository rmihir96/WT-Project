Why does MentorSpot exist?

MentorSpot.com is a website which helps people learn from many MOOCs available on the internet without losing motivation and getting personalized advice by mentors who are experts in their respective fields and also who reside near them. The website is built around the idea of collective learning and growth, where users help other users to learn and guide them through the duration of course. Thus the websites will have two types of users: Mentors who are expert in a particular field or course, and learners who are learning or intend to learn a particular course. It gives the students all the more reason to study a particular subject/ topic in detail besides sharing a quick view on the struggles of the Master. We at envisage to create a parallel learning system that is lesser theoretical and integrate it with their regular curriculum so that lack of option becomes the last deterrent for a student to pursue his/ her interest. Here at mentorspot we provide students various assignments and projects related to the course, it is made compulsory that every student should complete their assignments and projects. On basis of the course selected every student will be added in a group which will operated by the mentor of that course. Main purpose of MentorSpot is to bring a new revolution in the field of e-learning. It will also help to create a good relationship between mentor and the student. Student can get in video chatting room only if mentor agrees. Website will classify student based on their knowledge like beginner level, intermediate level, expert level .Assignment and projects will be based on the levels. After the completion of course student will be provided with certificates and letter of recommendation. After student completes the course he/she can apply for another course or can register as a mentor for the course he/she has completed. Mentors will not only help students to clear the doubts or assign them projects, but they will also motivate and guide students to compete in the challenging world. Special courses will be provided to mentors where they can learn from more experienced mentors. MentorSpot will help both student as well as mentor.

How to use this website?

Mentorspot is a friendly website, where anyone can use the website. MentorSpot consists of two types of user: Student and Mentor. Student is that type of user who want to learn from the website, utilize the resources, clear the doubts and many more. Mentor is that type of user who will help student to complete the by providing resources and complete guidance about the particular course. Registration/login Process : Register as student OR Register as Mentor. “Register as student”-Student just need to fill the online for, which will include name, email address , strong password for privacy , country and age. “Register as mentor” – mentor will register into website by providing email address ,password, speciality in particular course and experience on a particular course. After the registration will select the course . After course selection will select the course level for eg:-beginner, intermediate, expert. After registration as a mentor, Mentor will select the course type and duration time. Student will get added in a small group according to their course and mentor will be assigned to each group. Group will have various functions like both the user can get into chat room, video conference option will also be provided, users can share files etc. After completion of each level students will provided with test series related to course.
